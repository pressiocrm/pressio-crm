## FAQ Section

**Section headline:**
Questions you are probably asking

**Q: Is the free version actually a full CRM, or will I hit a paywall?**
A: The free version includes contacts, a deal pipeline, tasks, email sending, activity tracking, a dashboard, and Contact Form 7 integration. There are no limits on contacts or deals, no time trial, and no features that stop working after 14 days. Many businesses will never need Pro.

**Q: How is this different from Jetpack CRM or FluentCRM?**
A: Pressio CRM is pipeline-first — the kanban deal board is the core of the product, not a secondary feature. The interface is a modern Vue single-page app that feels fast and responsive. Jetpack CRM is contact-first with an older interface. FluentCRM is primarily an email marketing tool, not a sales pipeline tool. Different use cases — Pressio CRM is for closing deals, not sending newsletters.

**Q: Can I migrate from HubSpot, a spreadsheet, or another CRM?**
A: Yes. Export your contacts to CSV from your current tool, then import the CSV in Pressio CRM under Settings. The importer handles column mapping and duplicate detection by email address.

**Q: Will this slow down my website for visitors?**
A: No. Pressio CRM only loads its scripts and styles on its own admin pages. It adds nothing to your site's frontend. Visitors will not notice any difference.

**Q: Is my customer data sent to any external service?**
A: No. All data is stored in your WordPress database on your own server. Nothing is transmitted externally. You have full control and full ownership.

**Q: What happens if I install Pro later — do I lose my free data?**
A: No. Pro is an add-on that extends the free plugin. All your existing contacts, deals, tasks, and history remain exactly where they are. Pro adds features on top — it never replaces or migrates your data.
